Our Vision

Winners Campus Fellowship (WCF) is an extention of the youth Alive Fellowship, Living Faith Church (Winner's Chapel) with a burning mandate to raise a generation of kingdom influencers right from the higher institution
We are an Ark-bearing fellowship, carrying God's presence, power and presence into the academic space

Objectives

  • To provide a spiritual family for student fromo their home church
  • To groom and empower future leaders in ministry,buisness, politics, media e.t.c
  • To retain and integrate new converts into the Winners fold while they're in school
  • To preserve destinies on campus from corruption and destruction (Hosea 4:6)
  • To maintain spiritual fire among students and ensure continuity of the revival (Leviticus 6:12-13)
